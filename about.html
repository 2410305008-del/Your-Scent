Our Story

Born from Nature's
Finest Blooms

Your Scent was born from a simple dream — to bottle the most beautiful moments in nature and offer them as wearable art.

Founded in 2018 in the fragrant gardens of Jaipur, we craft every perfume with love, care, and only the purest natural ingredients sourced from around the world.

Our Philosophy

Fragrance is Emotion,
Bottled.

We believe a perfume is not just a scent — it's a memory, an emotion, a part of your identity. That is why we take extraordinary care in crafting every single bottle.

Each of our fragrances goes through months of development, testing with real people, and refining until we capture the exact emotion we're aiming for — from the freshness of a morning garden to the cool serenity of an ocean breeze.

We partner with ethical farmers in France, India, and Sri Lanka for our botanicals, and we never compromise on the quality of our raw materials.

Our Ingredients

Only the Purest

We hand-select every ingredient with the utmost care for quality and sustainability.

🌹

Bulgarian Rose

Sourced from the Rose Valley of Bulgaria — the most prized rose oil in the world, rich, warm and deeply floral.

🫧

Aqua Crystals

Marine-derived aquatic molecules that capture the pure, clean scent of ocean air and fresh spring water.

🍊

Sicilian Citrus

Cold-pressed orange and lemon peel from sun-drenched Sicilian groves for the brightest, most vibrant top notes.

💜

Provence Lavender

Authentic French lavender essential oil, harvested at peak bloom for maximum aromatic richness and longevity.

🌳

Sandalwood

Ethically harvested Indian sandalwood, providing a creamy, warm base note that beautifully anchors all our blends.

🌼

Jasmine Absolute

Steam-distilled jasmine from Tamil Nadu, India — intensely floral, rich and deeply feminine.
